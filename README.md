# Portfolio

Contains few examples and samples of my work.

I was a software engineer who turned into a product manager. I have 8+ years of experience building products in various domains. My skills, strengths and accomplishments are as follows:

Skills:

1. AGILE methodology & SCRUM framework:
Ran the daily stand-ups and sprints effectively. Let the sprint planning and product backlog grooming sessions. Used JIRA to write user stories and take story point estimates. Created & achieved team OKRs.

2. Product Vision & Strategy:
Came up with the product vision & strategy answering the "Why", "What", "Who" and "How" questions. Made sure that the strategy is aligned with business goals and customer needs. 

3. Data Analysis:
Analyzed both qualitative and quantitative(product KPIs and metrics) data to figure out trends in the data. Have used SQL to fetch the required data set and Power BI to visualize user's behavior. Made data driven decisions while conducting A/B testing. Performed RCAs(root cause analysis) by doing random sampling & analyzing large data sets.  

4. User Research:
Conducted customer interviews, surveys and usability tests to map out user's needs, pain points and desires. Executed user feedback loop before every release. 

5. Wire-Framing & UI/UX Understanding:
Translated ideas into workflows using Figma mockups and worked closely with designers on prototypes.

6. Competitor Analysis w.r.t funding, acquisitions, product features, development team, SWOT analysis, pricing, revenue etc.

7. Prioritization of product features based on a mix of t-shirt sizing and impact/effort matrix.

Strengths:

1. Collaboration: 
Worked closely with engineers, designers, QA, internal & external stakeholders, sales, customer support, customer success, legal and marketing.

2. Strategic Thinking:
Always made sure that the product strategy targets the company's business goal (a north start metric). Aligned team tasks to long tern strategic goals. Made sure that a target market/persona is in focus while building a product strategy. 

3. Communication:
Always had an open and iterative communication with all the stakeholders so that at all times everyone was aware of what the team has delivered and what the team is currently working on. Proactively gave sprint release demos to customer support, operations, customer success and other stakeholders. Created release notes & tutorial documents to be shared before every sprint release.

4. Empathy
Intuitively understood user's paint points or the needs of the team members. Worked out solutions that genuinely solved the problem of either our customers or of internal team members.

5. Leadership:
Mentored & managed a team of 1 business analyst and 2 product managers while I was working with Morningstar Inc. Inspired engineers to deliver what was required within the required time frame. 

6. Domain Knowledge: 
Good knowledge of domains such as IoT, logistics & supply chain, telematics, maps routing, fleet & shipment management systems, CRMs, integration with 3rd party APIs, ERP and warehouse management systems, B2B SaaS, B2C, workforce management systems & international payments.
